App development costs will vary greatly depending on the features you need and what devices you want your app developed for. An app that needs to work on IOS and Android will oftentimes cost twice as much. Adding features such as user registration, secure login and leveraging device specific features such as the camera will all increase the cost to develop an app. Needing custom API's developed or needing to leverage 3rd party API's also increase the app development costs.
